Meet the team behind Rise

Rise was the brainchild of Rob Oyston who developed the idea for the movement-based programme following a stay in hospital. Originally operating under the Mobi-Game brand, Rob successfully launched his programme in the care home sector where the vibrancy of classes and inclusivity of the activities won over the hearts and minds of residents and care home owners alike. Enter business, franchising and care sector experts Trevor and Sam Brocklebank, and Rise was born. The programme is ideally suited to franchising with standards set centrally but delivered at a local level by franchisees. Rob, Trevor and Sam are joined by a fantastic management team who share their passion.

  • Rob Oyston

    Rob is a qualified teacher, an experienced entrepreneur and a graduate of the Goldman Sachs 10,000 Small Businesses Programme. A keen sportsman, he came to fully appreciate the need for Rise services after a stay in hospital resulting from a serious illness. The experience led Rob to design and deliver positive experiences through physical activities for people, initially to elderly care home residents. Originally operating under the Mobi-Game name, Rob’s programme brings real benefits to participants whilst impressing the regulatory authorities looking for positive outcomes in care settings.

  • Trevor Brocklebank

    Trevor has built multiple businesses including Home Instead – one of the UK’s most successful home care franchise companies – which he built up to a turnover of more than £100m before selling in 2016. Rise joins a number of companies benefitting from Trevor’s experience and stewardship; all are purpose driven and make a difference to the lives of all involved. Trevor was voted one of the top ten most influential people in Social Care.

  • Sam Brocklebank

    As co-founder of Home Instead in the UK, Sam has a broad understanding of the social care sector and the need for meaningful activities for seniors. Like Trevor, she is passionate about making a difference to people’s lives and committed to developing companies that give back to society. Sam has a particular interest in those who work in the care sector and the need to empower them to deliver outstanding results for their clients. Sam is also passionate about sport and is actively involved supporting Cheshire Netball.

  • Sadie Boundy

    A franchise expert, Sadie has worked in the care industry for over 15 years. She is a specialist in managing change in a fast-growing franchise business whilst maintaining oversight of quality and standards. She worked alongside Trevor and Sam at Home Instead where she was part of the Senior Leadership Team.

  • Laura Harvey-Smith

    Laura is an expert in franchising learning and development and franchise consultancy. Her experience includes Director of Education and Training for the British Franchise Association, Sales Training Lead for P&O and Cunard, Professional Development Manager for ERA and General Manager, Auditel.

  • Vicki Martin

    Vicki has a wealth of experience in a variety of sectors and more recently in the franchise industry. With over 20 years working in administration, project management and with in-depth systems and organisational knowledge, Vicki will be providing expert support across all areas of the business.
