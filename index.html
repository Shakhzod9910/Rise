About Rise

There can be many reasons why people of all ages lose the ability to remain mobile and active – advancing years, illness, or injury to name but a few. At Rise we transform people’s confidence, as well as their mental, physical and emotional wellbeing – by adapting games, sports and dance to an individual’s physical ability or cognitive capacity no matter what their environment. If you work in the NHS or another health or social care organisation, including care homes, children’s care, community groups and home care agencies, Rise can help to enrich the day-to-day experience of your clients.

We can support your team to get participants moving, improving outcomes whilst fulfilling and surpassing regulatory compliance. Alternatively, if you think our services could benefit you or a loved one, please contact us to find out how you can participate in a one to one or group session. For those wishing to help us extend the reach of this exciting programme, we also offer franchise opportunities.
